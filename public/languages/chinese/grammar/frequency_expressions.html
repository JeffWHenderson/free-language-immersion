Frequency Expressions — 常常, 有时候, 从来不

Frequency words tell us how often something happens. In Chinese, frequency adverbs go before the verb — usually after the subject, and after time expressions if both appear. They cannot go after the verb the way some English adverbs can.

Frequency Words

Frequency + Verb Examples

  • 我每天练习中文。 Wǒ měitiān liànxí zhōngwén. — I practice Chinese every day.
  • 她经常加班。 Tā jīngcháng jiābān. — She often works overtime.
  • 我有时候画画。 Wǒ yǒu shíhou huàhuà. — I sometimes paint.
  • 他偶尔去跑步。 Tā ǒu'ěr qù pǎobù. — He occasionally goes running.
  • 我从来不迟到。 Wǒ cónglái bù chídào. — I never arrive late.

Counting Occurrences: 一次, 两次 and V + 一下

Use 次 (cì) after a number to count occurrences. Use V + 一下 to say you'll do something briefly or casually.

  • 我每周运动三次。 Wǒ měi zhōu yùndòng sān cì. — I exercise three times a week.
  • 你能帮我看一下吗？ Nǐ néng bāng wǒ kàn yīxià ma? — Could you take a quick look for me?
  • 我休息一下。 Wǒ xiūxi yīxià. — I'll take a quick break.
