会、能、可以 — Three Ways to Say "Can"

Chinese has three different words for "can," each with a distinct meaning.

会 (huì)能 (néng)可以 (kěyǐ)
Meaninglearned skill / abilityphysically able / circumstances allowpermitted / allowed
Englishknow how tobe able tomay / allowed to

会 — learned skill

  • 我会说中文。 I can speak Chinese. (I learned how)
  • 你会开车吗？ Can you drive? (do you know how?)
  • 我不会游泳。 I can't swim. (I never learned)

能 — circumstances/physical ability

  • 我今天不能来。 I can't come today. (circumstances)
  • 我能帮忙。 I am able to help.

可以 — permission

  • 我可以坐这里吗？ Can I sit here? (is it okay?)
  • 你可以走了。 You can go now. (you're allowed)
