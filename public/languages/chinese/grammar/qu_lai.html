去 & 来 — Go & Come

去 (qù) means to go — movement away from the speaker. 来 (lái) means to come — movement toward the speaker.

去 — to go

  • 我去学校。 I am going to school.
  • 你去哪里？ Where are you going?
  • 我明天去。 I'll go tomorrow.

来 — to come

  • 他来了。 He is coming. / He's here.
  • 请进来。 Please come in.
  • 你来吗？ Are you coming?

去/来 + place or verb

PatternExampleEnglish
去 + place我去市场。I'm going to the market.
去 + verb我去吃饭。I'm going to eat.
从...来他从北京来。He came from Beijing.

了 with 去/来

Adding 了 after 去 or 来 shows the action is completed or has just started:

  • 她去医院了。 She went to the hospital.
  • 他来了。 He's arrived / He came.
