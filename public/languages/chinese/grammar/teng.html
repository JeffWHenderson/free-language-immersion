疼 (téng) — To Hurt

In Chinese, describing pain is straightforward: body part + 疼. The pronoun (my/your) is usually omitted when the context is clear.

Basic structure

Subject + body part + 疼

  • 我头疼。 My head hurts.
  • 我肚子疼。 My stomach hurts.
  • 他腿疼。 His leg hurts.

General discomfort — 不舒服

不舒服 (bù shūfú) = uncomfortable / feeling unwell. Use it when you're not well but can't pinpoint the pain:

  • 我不舒服。 I feel unwell.
  • 我有点不舒服。 I feel a little off.

Asking about pain

  • 哪里疼？ Where does it hurt?
  • 很疼吗？ Does it hurt a lot?
