在 (zài) — Location

在 expresses where someone or something is. It can also indicate an ongoing action when placed before a verb.

Expressing location

Subject + 在 + place

  • 我在家。 I am at home.
  • 他在学校。 He is at school.
  • 餐厅在那里。 The restaurant is there.

Asking where

  • 你在哪里？ Where are you?
  • 银行在哪里？ Where is the bank?

在 + verb = ongoing action

在 placed before a verb shows the action is happening right now (similar to -ing in English):

  • 我在吃饭。 I am eating (right now).
  • 她在工作。 She is working.
  • 他在看书。 He is reading.

Negation

  • 她不在家。 She is not at home.
  • 我不在办公室。 I am not in the office.
