只要...就... — As Long As

只要 (zhǐyào) means "as long as" or "provided that." It introduces a sufficient condition — the one thing needed for the result to follow. The result clause uses 就 (jiù). The pattern implies that no matter what else is true, this single condition guarantees the outcome. It is one of the most motivating and practical sentence patterns in everyday Chinese.

Pattern: 只要 + Condition, 就 + Result

ChinesePinyinEnglish
只要努力，就会成功。Zhǐyào nǔlì, jiù huì chénggōng.As long as you work hard, you will succeed.
只要你来，我就高兴。Zhǐyào nǐ lái, wǒ jiù gāoxìng.As long as you come, I'll be happy.
只要坚持练习，就能进步。Zhǐyào jiānchí liànxí, jiù néng jìnbù.As long as you keep practicing, you can improve.
只要有时间，我就去帮你。Zhǐyào yǒu shíjiān, wǒ jiù qù bāng nǐ.As long as I have time, I'll go help you.

Comparing 只要...就... and 如果...就...

如果...就... sets up a simple if/then condition. 只要...就... is stronger — it says this one condition is both necessary and sufficient. The tone of 只要 is more reassuring or emphatic.

  • 如果你有问题，就问我。 Rúguǒ nǐ yǒu wèntí, jiù wèn wǒ. — If you have a question, ask me.
  • 只要有问题，就来问我。 Zhǐyào yǒu wèntí, jiù lái wèn wǒ. — Whenever you have a question — just come ask me.

Combining with Negation

只要 pairs naturally with negative conditions. The message is often reassuring: "as long as you don't do X, everything will be fine."

  • 只要不下雨，我们就去爬山。 Zhǐyào bù xià yǔ, wǒmen jiù qù pá shān. — As long as it doesn't rain, we'll go hiking.
  • 只要你不放弃，就一定能做到。 Zhǐyào nǐ bù fàngqì, jiù yīdìng néng zuò dào. — As long as you don't give up, you can definitely do it.

Practical Everyday Examples

  • 只要开心就好。 Zhǐyào kāixīn jiù hǎo. — As long as you're happy, that's all that matters.
  • 只要每天复习，就不会忘记。 Zhǐyào měitiān fùxí, jiù bù huì wàngjì. — As long as you review every day, you won't forget.
  • 只要大家齐心合力，就没有做不到的事。 Zhǐyào dàjiā qíxīn hélì, jiù méiyǒu zuò bù dào de shì. — As long as everyone works together, there's nothing that can't be done.
