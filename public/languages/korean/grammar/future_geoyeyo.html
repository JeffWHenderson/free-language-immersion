-(으)ㄹ 거예요 — Future & Intention

-(으)ㄹ 거예요 expresses future intention or prediction — equivalent to "will" or "going to" in English. Attach ㄹ 거예요 directly after vowel-ending stems and after ㄹ-ending stems; attach 을 거예요 after other consonant-ending stems. For deliberate intention, -(으)려고 해요 ("I'm planning to / I intend to") is also very common.

Formation

Stem endingEnding to addExampleMeaning
Vowel (가-)ㄹ 거예요갈 거예요will go
Vowel (하-)ㄹ 거예요할 거예요will do
Vowel (보-)ㄹ 거예요볼 거예요will see/watch
Consonant (먹-)을 거예요먹을 거예요will eat
Consonant (읽-)을 거예요읽을 거예요will read

-(으)려고 해요 — Intention / Plan

KoreanRomanizationEnglish
먹으려고 해요meogeuryogo haeyoI'm planning to eat
공부하려고 해요gongbuharyogo haeyoI'm planning to study
쉬려고 해요swuiryogo haeyoI'm planning to rest

Negation

Place 안 or 못 before the verb stem to negate the future form.

  • 안 갈 거예요. an gal geoyeyo — I'm not going to go.
  • 못 먹을 거예요. mot meogeul geoyeyo — I won't be able to eat.

Example Sentences

  • 내일 친구를 만날 거예요. naeil chingureul mannal geoyeyo — I'm going to meet a friend tomorrow.
  • 오늘 저녁에 공부할 거예요. oneul jeonyeoge gongbuhal geoyeyo — I'll study this evening.
  • 주말에 집에서 쉴 거예요. jumare jibeseo swil geoyeyo — I'll rest at home on the weekend.
