-(으)면 — If / When

-(으)면 is the main conditional connector in Korean, meaning "if" or "when." It attaches directly to the verb or adjective stem: use -으면 after a consonant-final stem, and -면 after a vowel-final stem. It covers both real conditions ("if X happens, Y") and habitual or universal truths ("when X, always Y").

Formation

Stem typeEndingExample verbConditional form
Consonant-final stem-으면먹다 (to eat)먹으면
Vowel-final stem-면피곤하다 (to be tired)피곤하면
Vowel-final stem-면행복하다 (to be happy)행복하면
Vowel-final stem-면오다 (to come)오면
ㄹ-final stem (ㄹ drops)-면알다 (to know)알면

Real Conditions — If X, Then Y

  • 피곤하면 쉬세요. pigonhamyeon swiseyo — If you're tired, please rest.
  • 시간이 있으면 같이 운동해요. sigani isseumyeon gachi undonghaeyo — If you have time, let's exercise together.
  • 스트레스를 받으면 음악을 들어요. seuteurereul badeumyeon eumageul deoreoyo — When I'm stressed, I listen to music.
  • 날씨가 좋으면 하이킹을 가요. nalssiga joeumyeon haikingeul gayo — If the weather is good, we go hiking.

Habitual / Universal Truths

  • 봄이 오면 꽃이 피어요. bomi omyeon kkochi pieoyo — When spring comes, flowers bloom.
  • 열심히 연습하면 실력이 늘어요. yeolsimhi yeonseupahmyeon sillyeogi neureoyo — If you practice hard, your skills improve.

-(으)면 좋겠어요 — Would Be Nice If / I Wish

  • 행복하면 좋겠어요. haengbokahmyeon joketsseoyo — I wish I were happy. / It would be nice to be happy.
  • 주말에 쉴 수 있으면 좋겠어요. jumare swil su isseumyeon joketsseoyo — It would be nice if I could rest on the weekend.
  • 기분이 나아지면 좋겠어요. gibuni naajimyeon joketsseoyo — I hope my mood gets better.

-(으)면 안 돼요 — Must Not / Not Allowed

  • 화가 나도 소리치면 안 돼요. hwaga nado sorichimyeon an dwaeyo — Even if you're angry, you must not shout.
  • 피곤해도 포기하면 안 돼요. pigonhaedo pogihomyeon an dwaeyo — Even if you're tired, you must not give up.

Mood & Emotion Context Examples

  • 슬프면 울어도 괜찮아요. seulpeumyeon ureodo gwaenchanayo — If you're sad, it's okay to cry.
  • 걱정이 있으면 말해요. geokjeogi isseumyeon malhaeyo — If you have worries, talk about them.
  • 기분이 좋으면 같이 노래해요. gibuni joeumyeon gachi noraehaeyo — If you're in a good mood, let's sing together.
