Le Conditionnel — The Conditional Mood

The conditional expresses polite requests, wishes, hypothetical situations, and advice. It is formed like the future tense but uses the imparfait endings.

Formation

Future stem (usually the infinitive, with -re verbs dropping the final -e) + imparfait endings:

SubjectEndingparler (stem: parler-)
je-aisje parlerais
tu-aistu parlerais
il / elle-aitil parlerait
nous-ionsnous parlerions
vous-iezvous parleriez
ils / elles-aientils parleraient

Uses of the Conditional

  • Je voudrais un café, s'il vous plaît. I would like a coffee, please. (polite request)
  • Pourriez-vous m'aider ? Could you help me? (formal polite request)
  • J'irais en France si j'avais le temps. I would go to France if I had the time. (hypothetical)
  • Tu devrais te reposer. You should rest. (advice)
  • Il aimerait être médecin. He would like to be a doctor. (wish)

Conditional in si Clauses

In hypothetical sentences: si + imparfait → conditional. Never use the conditional in the si clause itself.

  • Si j'avais de l'argent, je voyagerais. If I had money, I would travel.
  • Si tu voulais, tu pourrais réussir. If you wanted to, you could succeed.
