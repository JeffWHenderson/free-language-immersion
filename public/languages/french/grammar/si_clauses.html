Si — If Clauses & Conditionals

Si clauses express conditions — what happens if something else is true. French uses specific tense combinations depending on whether the condition is real and possible, or hypothetical and contrary to fact. The tense in the si clause determines the tense in the result clause.

Type 1 — Real Condition (Present / Likely)

The condition is possible or likely. Use si + présent → result in futur simple or présent:

Si clause (condition)Result clauseEnglish
Si tu travailles dur,tu réussiras.If you work hard, you will succeed.
Si elle est stressée,elle se repose.If she is stressed, she rests.
Si nous partons tôt,nous arriverons à l'heure.If we leave early, we will arrive on time.

Type 2 — Hypothetical Condition (Unreal / Imagined)

The condition is imaginary or unlikely. Use si + imparfait → result in conditionnel présent:

Si clause (imparfait)Result clause (conditionnel)English
Si j'avais plus de temps,je serais moins stressé.If I had more time, I would be less stressed.
Si elle était médecin,elle aiderait plus de gens.If she were a doctor, she would help more people.
Si nous habitions à Paris,nous parlerions mieux français.If we lived in Paris, we would speak French better.

Tense Summary

TypeSi clauseResult clause
Real / likelysi + présentfutur simple or présent
Hypothetical / unrealsi + imparfaitconditionnel présent
Impossible / past regretsi + plus-que-parfaitconditionnel passé

Important: Never Use the Future or Conditional After Si

Unlike English "if I will" or "if I would," French never uses the futur or conditionnel directly after si in conditional clauses. The imparfait and plus-que-parfait are the correct choices for unreal conditions.

Example Sentences with Emotion Context

  • Si j'avais plus de temps, je serais moins stressé. If I had more time, I would be less stressed.
  • Si tu es triste, parle-moi. If you are sad, talk to me.
  • Si elle se reposait davantage, elle se sentirait mieux. If she rested more, she would feel better.
  • Si seulement il était là ! If only he were here!
  • Si jamais tu te sens seul, appelle-moi. If ever you feel lonely, call me.
  • Même si c'est difficile, je continuerai. Even if it's difficult, I will continue.
