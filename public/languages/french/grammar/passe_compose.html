Passé Composé — The Conversational Past

The passé composé is the main past tense in spoken French. It describes completed actions in the past. It is formed with a helper verb (avoir or être) + the past participle of the main verb.

Forming the Past Participle

Infinitive endingParticiple endingExample
-er-éparler → parlé
-ir-ifinir → fini
-re-uvendre → vendu

Negation in Passé Composé

Place ne...pas around the helper verb, not the participle.

  • Je n'ai pas mangé. I didn't eat.
  • Elle n'a pas vu le film. She didn't see the film.
  • Tu n'as pas fini ? You didn't finish?
