Ser — Identity & Descriptions

Spanish has two verbs for "to be": ser and estar. Use ser for identity — things that are permanent or defining about a person or thing.

When to use ser

UseExampleEnglish
NameMe llamo Ana. / Soy Ana.My name is Ana.
OriginSoy de España.I'm from Spain.
NationalitySomos americanos.We're American.
ProfessionÉl es médico.He is a doctor.
CharacteristicsElla es inteligente.She is intelligent.
RelationshipsEs mi amigo.He is my friend.

No article before professions

  • Él es médico. He is a doctor. (not: un médico)
  • Soy profesora. I am a teacher.

Adjective agreement

Adjectives must agree with the noun in gender and number:

  • Soy americano. I am American. (masculine)
  • Soy americana. I am American. (feminine)
